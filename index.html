About Band of Bookworms

We are a humble band of homeschoolers who simply love to read. Brought together by a shared curiosity and a love for stories, we meet regularly to connect, chat, and journey through books that spark imagination and thoughtful conversation.

Sometimes we explore timeless classics like Pride and Prejudice, and other times we dive into modern reads like Brave or Land of Stories.

Our Mission

We, the people of Band of Bookworms, declare our mission is to aim re-ignite a lifelong passion for reading by creating a welcoming and inspiring community for book lobing homeschoolers. We believe in the power of stories to open new worlds, foster curiosity, and spark meaningful conversations, all while having fun and making lifelong bonds.
