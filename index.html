About ChapterX

We are a humble band of homeschoolers who simply love to read. Brought together by a shared curiosity and a love for stories, we meet regularly to connect, chat, and journey through books that spark imagination and thoughtful conversation.

Sometimes we explore timeless classics like Pride and Prejudice, and other times we dive into famous modern reads like Brave or Land of Stories. Whatever the book, each one brings fresh ideas to consider, in which we can discuss as a group, while having fun with it!

Our book club is created by and for homeschoolers in Fairview, and it’s open to all who enjoy reading and sharing that joy with others. Whether you're a seasoned bookworm or just starting to fall in love with reading, you’re welcome here.
